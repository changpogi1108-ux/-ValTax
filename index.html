    Guide to eBIRForms

    1. Download and install the latest eBIRForms package from the BIR website.
    2. Open the application and select the appropriate form (e.g., 1701, 1702).
    3. Fill out taxpayer details such as TIN, RDO code, and taxpayer name.
    4. Input income, deductions, and tax due amounts carefully.
    5. Click "Validate" to check for errors before submission.
    6. Submit online directly through the app or save as a file to upload later.
    7. Print or save the email confirmation for your records.

    Guide to eFPS

    1. Visit the eFPS website.
    2. Log in with your registered TIN and password.
    3. Select the applicable form (VAT, ITR, withholding tax, etc.).
    4. Fill out the form fields as required.
    5. Validate and submit the form.
    6. Proceed to payment by selecting your enrolled bank.
    7. Save and print the payment confirmation for compliance proof.

    Guide to eAFS

    1. Go to the eAFS portal.
    2. Log in with your eFPS credentials.
    3. Prepare your scanned copies of financial statements, receipts, and attachments.
    4. Upload files according to the required categories (FS, QAP, SAWT, etc.).
    5. Confirm upload and check status in the dashboard.
    6. Download the reference number and retain it as proof of submission.

    Guide to ORUS

    1. Visit the ORUS portal.
    2. Create an account using your TIN and registered email.
    3. Verify your email and log in.
    4. Select the type of online registration (e.g., ATP, COR updates).
    5. Fill in the required fields and upload supporting documents.
    6. Submit the application and wait for confirmation via email.
    7. Download the approved certificate/document once processed.

    Troubleshooting Common Issues

    eBIRForms Issues

    • Problem: App won't install.
      Fix: Ensure Java is updated. Download latest version from BIR site.
    • Problem: Validation errors.
      Fix: Double-check TIN and RDO codes. Use official taxpayer info.

    eFPS Issues

    • Problem: Cannot log in.
      Fix: Reset password via "Forgot Password" or contact RDO eFPS helpdesk.
    • Problem: Payment not reflecting.
      Fix: Check bank enrollment status; contact bank support if delayed.

    eAFS Issues

    • Problem: Upload fails.
      Fix: Compress files into .zip and ensure under size limit.
    • Problem: Missing attachments.
      Fix: Follow correct naming convention and re-upload files.

    ORUS Issues

    • Problem: Registration not saving.
      Fix: Use updated browser (Chrome/Edge) and clear cache.
    • Problem: Email verification not received.
      Fix: Check spam folder or request resend from ORUS portal.
